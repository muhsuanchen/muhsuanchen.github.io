Way Out

NTU  |  Jan. 2017 - May. 2017
ACM CHI' 17 Student Game Competition, Denver, Colorado, USA.
In the game, the player can walk through a panoramic forest maze that consists of four layers in depth and drag items in physical 3D space.
- Design and built multi-layer 360 panorama scenes and gaming functions. (Unity3D)
- Exhibition and presentation at Denver, America for 2 days.

  Runner-up.
